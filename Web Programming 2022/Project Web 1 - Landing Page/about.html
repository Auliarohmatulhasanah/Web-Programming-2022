About Restaurant Italian

Restaurant Italian in Lenmarc Mall, West Surabaya.Established in August 2016, we have been serving authentic Italian menus; Pizza, Pasta, and Salad. In March 2019 , Restaurant Italian open the 2nd branch in Denpasar, Bali.Restaurant Italian has been praised for its extensive wine collection and is the destination for middle- to upper-class youth to enjoy wine with friends. With the strong figure of Chef Juna Rorimpandey, chef Arnold Poernomo and Renatta Moeloek. We have become a place for families, friends and companies to enjoy their meals ranging from casual eats to special events, such as birthday celebrations and company gatherings.
